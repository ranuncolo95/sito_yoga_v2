Our Offerings

Practice Modalities

Hatha Yoga

All Levels 60-90 min sessions

The foundation of all yoga practices focusing on proper alignment, breath control, and building strength through sustained postures.

Key Benefits:

  • Improves flexibility and joint health
  • Builds core strength and stability
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Enhances mind-body connection

Vinyasa Flow

Intermediate 75 min sessions

Dynamic sequences linking movement with breath in creative flows that build heat, endurance, and fluidity.

Key Benefits:

  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Increases stamina and endurance
  • Enhances mental focus and clarity
  • Builds full-body strength

Ashtanga Yoga

Advanced 90-120 min sessions

A rigorous style following a specific sequence of postures synchronized with breath to produce internal heat and detoxification.

Key Benefits:

  • Builds intense physical stamina
  • Promotes discipline and focus
  • Detoxifies muscles and organs
  • Develops advanced strength and flexibility

Pilates Matwork

All Levels 60 min sessions

A system of exercises using body weight to develop core strength, flexibility, and balanced muscle development.

Key Benefits:

  • Strengthens deep core muscles
  • Improves postural alignment
  • Enhances body awareness
  • Rehabilitates injuries

Meditation & Mindfulness

All Levels 30-60 min sessions

Techniques to cultivate present-moment awareness, reduce stress, and develop a calm, focused mind.

Key Benefits:

  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Improves emotional regulation
  • Enhances focus and concentration
  • Promotes better sleep
