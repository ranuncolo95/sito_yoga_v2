Perdèpera Resort Retreat

October 5-12, 2025 · Cardedu

Reserve Your Spot Explore Itinerary
Journey Into Nature

Immersive Experience

Join us for an unforgettable retreat at the stunning Perdepera Resort, set on Sardinia’s unspoiled east coast. Enjoy a fully planned week of Yoga and Pilates, with morning and afternoon classes held in beautiful, nature-immersed spots throughout the resort. Includes a welcome dinner, daily breakfast and dinner, vineyard wine tasting with local guides who speaks English. Last but not least, we will sail along the wild coves of Baunei, all the way to Cala Goloritzé and Cala Mariolu—famous for their turquoise waters and breathtaking scenery of the Mediterranean Sea.
