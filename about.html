Our Journey

Rooted in tradition, growing with innovation

Our Philosophy INSPIRED BY SARDINIA, CREATED FOR YOUR SOUL

Zen Island Retreat is the natural evolution of our personal and professional paths, a reflection of who we are and where we come from. We were born and raised in Sardinia, where nature is not a backdrop but a presence that shapes every breath and every step. Growing up between the sea and mountain, we learned early the value of simplicity, presence and connection. Years later, as certified Yoga and Pilates instructors, we found ourselves called to create something more than classes - a space for transformation. A place where movement meets meaning, and where modern practices can coexist with ancient rhythms. This retreat is our way of honouring our roots while growing forward. A shared vision shaped by friendship, inspired by our homeland and created with intention for you to reconnect, realign and rise.

Meet Your Guides

Master Instructors

VERONICA

Yoga Instructor Expert

Originally from Sardinia and now based in Ireland, Veronica is the cofounder of Zen Island Retreat — a project born from the desire to reconnect with her roots and share the healing rhythm of her homeland. She is a qualified midwife in one of Ireland’s busiest maternity hospitals, and holds a Master’s degree in pelvic floor rehabilitation. Her work in such a high-intensity environment has deepened her understanding of the body, resilience, and the need for balance on all levels. A certified yoga teacher with Yoga Alliance, Veronica is also trained in prenatal and postnatal yoga and Hypnobirthing. Her teaching blends clinical insight with breath-led, intentional movement — with a strong focus on alignment, pelvic floor awareness, and long-term wellbeing. With empathy and attention to detail, she creates safe, supportive spaces where each student feels empowered to move mindfully, build strength, and connect deeply to their body — on and off the mat.

FEDERICA

Pilates Instructor Expert

Federica has always been passionate about movement and the human body. Growing up in Sardinia, she developed a love for sports, especially beach volleyball, which inspired her to pursue a career in Physiotherapy. Now based in London, Federica is a qualified Physiotherapist with experience in musculoskeletal rehabilitation and injury prevention. She holds certifications in Mat and Reformer Pilates from the APPI (Australian Physiotherapy & Pilates Institute), and integrates her physiotherapy background into dynamic yet acce­ssible 1:1 and group sessions. Federica is the cofounder of Zen Island Retreat, a project that brings together her professional skills and her deep connection to the rhythms of her homeland. Federica combines her expertise in musculoskeletal rehabilitation with a preventative approach rooted in Pilates. She offers a safe and supportive environment for participants dealing with past or current injuries, providing targeted sessions that aim not only to rehabilitate but also to prevent future issues.

Our Offerings

Practice Modalities

Hatha Yoga

All Levels 60 min sessions

Hatha Yoga is a traditional and balanced practice that combines physical postures (āsana), breath regulation (prāṇāyāma), and mindful awareness. It is a slower-paced style that emphasizes alignment, stillness and presence, making it accessible to all levels.

Key Benefits:

  • Builds strength, stability and flexibility
  • Supports healthy posture and joint functiony
  • Calms the mind and balances the nervous system
  • Develops breath awareness and inner focus

Vinyasa Flow

All levels 45-60 min sessions

Vinyasa Flow is a dynamic style of yoga where movement is seamlessly linked with the breath. Sequences are creative, fluid, and intentionally structured to build heat, focus and physical vitality.

Key Benefits:

  • Enhances cardiovascular and muscular endurance
  • Improves mobility, coordination and balance
  • Encourages mental clarity and stress release
  • Fosters a deep sense of flow and embodiment

Yoga Nidra

All levels 30-40 min sessions

Yoga Nidra, or “yogic sleep,” is a guided meditation practice that leads the body into deep relaxation while keeping the mind gently aware. Practiced lying down, it is a powerful tool for nervous system restoration and emotional balance.

Key Benefits:

  • Promotes deep physical and mental rest
  • Reduces anxiety, fatigue, and insomnia
  • Supports emotional resilience and clarity
  • Awakens a deeper state of inner calm and insight

Pilates Matwork

All Levels 45-60 min sessions

Pilates Matwork is a precise and controlled movement system designed to strengthen the core, support spinal health, and improve overall body awareness. Grounded in breath and alignment, it is both rehabilitative and strengthening.

Key Benefits:

  • Strengthens deep abdominal and postural muscles
  • Improves posture, joint stability and balance
  • Aids in injury prevention and recovery
  • Promotes functional strength and mobility
